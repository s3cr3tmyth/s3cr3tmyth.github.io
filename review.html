Review

Fleet Foxes's second album! I actually bought this album a while ago.

Almost a year ago! And for some reason I have NEVER listen to it all the way through.... until last night. I was very surprised!

I cound not have imagined this album being actually better than the first! Well right now I am debateing to myself which album is better cause it is very hard.

The sound has improved from the first album. There is a lot more instruments on this album like violins, flute, and even a free jazz saxophone that kind of

reminds me of a Canterbury scene band like Soft Machine or Matching Mole. Also my favorite improvment for this album is the vocals!
